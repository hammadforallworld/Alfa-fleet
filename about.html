EST. 2006

18 Years of White-Glove Service

Founded in New York City, APEX Chauffeur was built to solve a simple problem: the lack of consistent, high-standard executive transportation. While ridesharing platforms introduced convenience, they compromised on safety, predictability, and professionalism.

We chose a different path. We decided to invest in our vehicles, train our chauffeurs as career professionals under strict NDAs, and guarantee transparent, flat-rate pricing without surge increases.

Today, APEX services 47+ major metropolitan markets across the United States, keeping the same core values that we started with: privacy, precision, and punctuality.

  • NDA-trained, background-checked career chauffeurs
  • DOT licensed & commercially insured ($1M+ liability policy)
  • Defensive driving certified & background screened every 2 years
  • Fleet cleaned, disinfected, and inspected before every single ride
